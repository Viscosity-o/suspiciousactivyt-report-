You are a **senior UI/UX designer specializing in enterprise fintech and compliance platforms used by global banks like Barclays**.

Your task is to design:

1. A **Landing Page**
2. A **Sign-In Page**

for a product called:

**“SAR Generator – AI-powered Suspicious Activity Report System”**

⚠️ STRICT RULES:

* The design must match a **Barclays-style corporate website** (clean, structured, professional)
* Use **existing Barclays-like color palette (deep blue, white, minimal accent colors)**
* Avoid playful UI, gradients, or startup-style flashy elements
* Maintain **enterprise compliance tool aesthetic**
* Layout should feel like a **banking product, not a SaaS landing page**
