You are a world-class document designer specializing in financial compliance reports. Generate a **professional, print-ready PDF layout** for a Suspicious Activity Report (SAR) used in banking systems.

🎯 Goal:
Create a SAR document that looks like an **official legal/compliance report**, suitable for submission to regulators, with a clean, structured, paragraph-based format (NO tables).

---

🎨 Design Requirements:

* Paper Size: A4 (or Letter 8.5 x 11)
* Margins: 1 inch on all sides
* Font: Times New Roman or similar serif font
* Font Size:

  * Headings: 14–16 pt (bold)
  * Body: 12 pt
  * Footnotes/Footer: 10 pt
* Colors:

  * Black text on white background
  * Minimal use of dark blue for headings (optional)
* Alignment:

  * Justified text for body
  * Left-aligned headings
* Line spacing: 1.5
* Section spacing: consistent and professional

---

📄 DOCUMENT STRUCTURE:

🔷 HEADER (Top of Page)

* Title: **SUSPICIOUS ACTIVITY REPORT** (centered, bold, uppercase)
* Below title:

  * Report Reference
  * Case Reference
  * Date of Report
  * Classification: STRICTLY CONFIDENTIAL

---

🔹 1. Subject Details
Write in **formal paragraph format**:
Include:

* Customer name, reference ID
* DOB, nationality
* Account details
* KYC risk
* PEP status
* Account status and balance

---

🔹 2. Grounds for Suspicion

📌 2.1 Overview

* Paragraph explaining why the report is raised

📌 2.2 Transaction Activity

* Paragraph summarizing transactions (NO TABLES)
* Mention:

  * Number of transactions
  * Amount range
  * Dates
  * Counterparties

📌 2.3 Pattern Analysis

* Paragraph explaining:

  * Structuring behavior
  * Repetition of amounts
  * Timing anomalies

📌 2.4 Absence of Legitimate Explanation

* Paragraph explaining lack of valid justification

---

🔹 3. Financial Summary

* Paragraph summarizing:

  * Total value
  * Average transaction
  * Standard deviation
  * Debit vs credit
  * Behavioral insights

---

🔹 4. Regulatory & Legal Terms

* Formal legal paragraph including references to:

  * Proceeds of Crime Act (POCA 2002)
  * Money Laundering Regulations (MLR 2017)
  * NCA SAR Guidelines
  * JMLSG Guidance
* Include:

  * Good faith disclosure statement
  * Tipping-off prohibition notice

---

🔹 5. Analyst Declaration

* Formal statement paragraph:

  * Analyst confirms accuracy
* Include:

  * Analyst Name
  * Analyst ID
  * Date of Review
  * Signature line (blank line)
  * Authorising Manager (blank line)

---

🔻 FOOTER (Every Page)

* Format:
  Report Ref | Case Ref | Page X of Y | STRICTLY CONFIDENTIAL
* Small font (10 pt)
* Light separator line above footer

---

💡 Additional Instructions:

* Use proper legal tone (formal, objective, precise)
* Maintain paragraph-based readability
* Avoid bullet points and tables
* Ensure content flows like a legal report
* Add page numbers automatically

---

🎯 Output:
Generate a clean, structured SAR document ready for PDF export that looks like an official banking compliance report.
